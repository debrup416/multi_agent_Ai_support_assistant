# Account and sign-in help

Manage your profile, password, and sign-in from **Account → Settings**.

- **Reset password:** use **Forgot password** on the sign-in screen to receive a reset
  link by email.
- **Update email:** change your account email under **Account → Settings → Email**;
  you'll confirm the change via a verification link.
- **Sign out everywhere:** if you think someone else has access, use **Sign out of all
  devices** and then reset your password.
- **Profiles:** add or remove viewing profiles for members of your household.

For account changes we can't complete here — like closing your account — ask to speak
with a human and we'll open a support ticket.
