# Streaming and supported devices

Titles marked as streaming-available can be watched instantly on supported devices;
other titles are available as rentals.

- **Where to watch:** stream in your web browser, or on our mobile and TV apps.
- **Offline:** downloads for offline viewing are available on the mobile app for
  streaming-available titles.
- **Quality:** streaming quality adapts to your connection; a stable connection of at
  least 5 Mbps is recommended for HD.
- **Availability:** a title's streaming availability can change over time. Search the
  catalog to check whether a specific film is currently available to stream.

If a title won't play, sign out and back in, check your connection, and try again.
