# Billing, renewals, and refunds

Subscriptions renew automatically on your billing date using your default payment
method. You can see your next renewal date under **Account → Billing**.

- **Auto-renew:** turn auto-renew on or off from the billing page. With it off, your
  plan ends at the current period's end date.
- **Refunds:** refund eligibility depends on your region and how recently you were
  charged. Refunds and cancellations are reviewed by a support agent — for your
  security they are not processed automatically by the assistant.
- **Failed payments:** we retry a failed renewal for several days before pausing
  streaming. Update your payment method to resume access.

To request a cancellation or refund, ask to speak with a human and we'll open a ticket.
