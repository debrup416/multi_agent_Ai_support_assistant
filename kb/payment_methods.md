# Updating your payment method

You can update the payment method on your account at any time.

1. Sign in and open **Account → Billing**.
2. Select **Payment methods**.
3. Choose **Add card** to add a new credit or debit card, or **Edit** to update an
   existing card's expiry or billing address.
4. Set a card as **Default** to use it for your next subscription renewal.

Changes to your payment method take effect on your next billing cycle. If a renewal
payment fails, we retry the charge for a few days before pausing streaming access.
For help with a declined card, contact support and we can look into it with you.
